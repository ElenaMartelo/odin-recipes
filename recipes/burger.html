Egg, ham and banana burger

Description

"Hideous" but delicious, a quick and fun burger with different textures and flavor profiles, for the most refined of taste.

Ingredients

  • 150g of minced meat
  • Two eggs
  • Thinly sliced bacon
  • A whole banana
  • A slice of cheese
  • A slice of ham
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 50mg of Breadcrumbs
  • A hambuger bun
  • One sliced tomato
  • Lettuce
  • Mayo
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Minced garlic

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix your meat with your bacon, salt, pepper, breadcrumbs and one egg, then form a patty shape, making a hole in them middle with your thumb.
  2. Cut your bun in half, butter it up and toast it until it turns golden brown.
  3. Get a pan, add olive oil, high heat, let it heat up for a while, add the patty to the pan and let it cook throughly on both sides.
  4. Meanwhile in a small bowl goes two spoonfulls of mayo, a tablespoon of ketchup and mustard, add your minced garlic and mix it up.
  5. On the side, slice your banana thinly and make a friend egg.
  6. Take your toasted bun, spread your mayo/ketchup/mustard on both sides, add your lettuce and a tomato slice to the top bun.
  7. When the patty is cooked, add a slice of cheese and ham on top of it, take the lid and pour some water on it, take the lid back to the pan, close it and let the cheese and ham melt. When its done, add your patty to the bottom bun, on top of it some banana slices, add the egg, more banana slices and close the burger.
