Spaghetti and Meatballs

Description

Easy to cook and easy to love, a well seasoned tomato sauce with tender meatballs, spaghetti al dente and parmesan cheese is the perfect recipe to eat alone or with others.

Ingredients

  • 500g of minced meat
  • 50ml of olive oil
  • 5 cloves of minced garlic
  • One diced onion
  • 500g of tomato sauce
  • Half a cup of water
  • 100g of parmesan cheese
  • 500g of spaghetti
  • A tablespoon of milk
  • Two egg
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 50g of breadcrumbs
  • A tablespoon of red chili flakes

Steps

  1. Pre-heat your oven at 200Â°C for twenty minutes.
  2. In a bowl, add your minced meat, milk, eggs, salt, pepper and breadcrumbs. Mix the ingredients with your hands until you can form meatballs without them breaking.
  3. In a baking sheet, add your meatballs and give it a glaze of olive oil. Put them in the oven and let them cook for 15 to 20 minutes.
  4. In a cooking pot, add your olive oil and your diced onion, let them turn golden, next add your minced garlic and your chili flakes, lowering the heat so they can cook without burning.
  5. After a few minutes, add your tomato sauce and stir the pot, turn your heat to medium, let it cook for a few minutes before adding your water.
  6. When the meatballs are done, add them to the sauce, lower the heat and close the lid on the pot, the sauce should cook for another 20 minutes or so.
  7. Meanwhile, bring your water to a boil, adding salt in the pot just before it starts to boil.
  8. Put your noodles in the water and give them a stir until they are softining up and not stucking together.
  9. Cook the spaghetti until al dente and then strain it, rinsing it on cold water so it stops it's cooking.
  10. With the noodles and sauce done, add the spaghetti to the sauce pan and give it a good stir, making sure the entire pasta is coated in sauce, check for salt.
  11. On your plate, add the spaghetti, the meatballs and a bit of parmesan cheese on top.
